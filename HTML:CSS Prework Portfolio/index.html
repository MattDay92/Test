Matt Day

Composer/Lyricist/Guitarist

Musician

Matt Day is a musical theatre composer/lyricist and professional guitarist currently based out of Indianapolis, IN.

After graduating from Indiana University, I began work on composing my first musical, Paper Swords, which was an award winner at the 2020 Chicago Musical Theatre Festival. My other work includes Make Me A Match (Allen & Gray New Voices Concert Series, IndyFringe 2019 Audience Choice Award Winner), Boy Meets Musical, and a few other projects still in early development. I recently released my first album, Different Lens, containing songs from Paper Swords and Make Me A Match and featuring performances by some of my closest friends.

Teacher

Currently, I teach Algebra 1 and Algebra 2 at Franklin Community High School in Franklin, IN.

I graduated from Indiana University in Bloomington, IN in May 2015 with a Bachelors of Science Degree in Mathematics Education. This is my eighth full year as a mathematics teacher in Indiana and my second at Franklin Community High School. I have taught Algebra 1, Geometry, Algebra 2, and Trigonometry.

Family

In October 2020, my wife Kelly and I got married. Kelly is a choir teacher and we met in fall of 2017 while we were both working at North Montgomery High School in Crawfordsville, IN and we still work together to this day. We live in Greenwood, IN and have two cats named Hamilton and Dexter.
